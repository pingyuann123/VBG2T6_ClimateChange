Global Temperature from 1990-2022

Global temperature trends from 1990 to 2022 highlight the gradual rise in average temperatures. These changes have been linked to human-driven factors such as increased greenhouse gas emissions and deforestation. Understanding these trends is crucial for forecasting future climate scenarios and implementing mitigation strategies.

UHI Intensity and Regional Impacts

Urban Heat Island (UHI) effects have significant regional impacts, especially in cities with dense populations and extensive concrete surfaces. The data reveals that nighttime UHI is a growing concern, exacerbating health risks during heat waves. Mitigating UHI requires strategic urban planning and increased green spaces.
